Bite Buddy — Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 30, 2026

Overview

Bite Buddy is a meal companion app designed for children. We take your family's privacy seriously. This policy explains how the app handles information.

Data Collection

We do not collect any data. Bite Buddy:

  • Does not collect personal information
  • Does not collect usage analytics
  • Does not use tracking or advertising
  • Does not use cookies
  • Does not connect to the internet
  • Does not transmit any data to any server

Local Data Only

All app data (meal progress, bite counts) exists only in device memory during active use. No data is saved to disk, and all information is lost when the app is closed. There is no account system, no login, and no cloud storage.

Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

Bite Buddy is designed for use by children. We comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and similar international regulations. Since we collect absolutely no data from any user — including children — there is no personal information to protect, share, or delete.

Third-Party Services

Bite Buddy does not integrate with any third-party services, SDKs, analytics platforms, or advertising networks.

Permissions

Bite Buddy does not request any special device permissions. It does not access the camera, microphone, location, contacts, or any other sensitive device features.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever update this privacy policy, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Any future versions of the app that collect data will require explicit consent.
